What is the important in your life?

This parallel coordinates visualization is designed to explore how do people feel about basic object (eg. family, friend, religion) in different country. I use data "the percentage of people who think highly of" to show this feeling. Additionally, attributes of country (eg. Income per person, birth rate, urban population) are showed as well, assisting in observing relationship among country and citizens' feeling.

Relation Visualization in 2005-2009

Select the region you interest

Explanation of each axis

    Country Attributes

  • Employee : Percentage of all labor that earns a salary from working.
  • Income : Income per person. Gross Domestic Product per capita in constant 2000 US$. The inflation but not the differences in the cost of living between countries has been taken into account.
  • Birth : Crude birth rate (births per 1000 population)
  • Urban : Urban population (% of total), which refers to people living in urban areas as defined by national statistical offices.
  • Young Labor : Age 25-54 labor to population (%). For age group 25-54, percentage of all labor to total population.
  • Old Labor : Age 65+ labor to population (%). For age group 65+, percentage of all labor to total population.

    • How do people feel

  • Family : The percentage of who think family is very important in life.
  • Friend : The percentage of who think friend is very important in life.
  • Leisure : The percentage of who think leisure time is very important in life.
  • Politics : The percentage of who think politics is very important in life.
  • Work : The percentage of who think work is very important in life.
  • Religion : The percentage of who think religion is very important in life.
  • Happiness : The percentage of who feel very happy about life.
  • Health : The percentage of who feel very healthy.
  • Satisfication : The percentage of who are very satisfy to life.
  • Freedom : The percentage of who feel very freedom in the country.
